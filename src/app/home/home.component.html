Pogocodes is the best online tool for finding PokemonGO friends to trade gifts and pokemon with and to challege the raid battles. You can quickly join the website and allow it to access your location to instantly get a list of nearby available trainers! Back in June 2018 Niantic added a feature to PokemonGO that allows trainers to become friends on the app and enjoy a variety of bonuses and challenges. Pogocodes was created so trainers will be able to easily find each other - whether you're searching for trainers that live right next to you or someone from the other side of the world! Why should I have friends on PokemonGO? Gifts - Each trainer obtains gifts from spinning poke-stops and gyms. Sending gifts to your friends grants them free items and increase your friendship level - which in turn increases many bonuses and reduces stardust amounts for trades! Giving and recieving gifts is one of the best features of PokemonGO. Trades - Once you're within 100 meters (~300 feet) of one of your friends you can trade pokemon with them. Help them expand their pokedex while you expand yours! But that's not all - trading pokemon has a chance of making the traded pokemon "lucky" - which yields even more bonuses later on. Raids - Want to catch those legendary pokemon with 50k+ CP? You are probably going to need some help with that! Team up with other trainers and challenge the raid battles to achieve unique and rare pokemon. Make sure to trade lot's of gifts so your attack bonus grows! Battles - Challenge your friends for a battle! You can win prizes if you win and make progress on your battle medals! Training with your friends and choosing the right Battle Party will improve your chances in the GO Battle League! How do I become friends with someone on PokemonGO? Easy! Copy any trainer code shown on this website by clicking on it. In PokemonGo tap the Avatar icon on the bottom left side of the screen then tap "Friends" on the top right. Once there, tap the "Add Friend" button and paste the trainer code in the input field. Click "Send" to finish. What's now? Your friend request has been sent to the user. Once they approve it you'll get a notification saying you are now friends with that user. If you're logged-in to PokemonGO on another device it's even easier - tap "QR CODE" on your "add friend" screen and scan the code of the trainer you want to add. That's it - a friend request will be sent immediately! To learn more about the Friendship Levels in PokemonGO and their bonuses click here. Happy trading!
